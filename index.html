H4

Capability, made legible.

Shae McKenna 2026

From the time I was ten.

Online platforms. Years of self-directed study.

By the time I was sixteen, I'd been to Harvard, MIT, and LSE.

Those experiences changed my life. None changed my resume.

Online education won.

Coursera. edX. MITx. Free knowledge for all.

Anyone could learn anything.

Universities won harder.

More expensive. Harder to get into.

Stanford. Harvard. MIT — more valuable than ever.

Education was never just instruction.

It was the credential.

And the credential is trust.

The problem isn't delivering knowledge.

It's making capability legible.

Credentials are compressed summaries of learning.

AI lets us observe the learning itself.
